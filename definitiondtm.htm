DEFINITION : DTM

 Top  Previous  Next

 

DTM stands for Digital Terrain Model. It is a collection of 3 dimensional co-ordinates, surveyed in such a manner that in combination, when modeled by a network of triangles, would represent a topographical terrain in the computer.

 

The DTM and survey modeling part of Model Maker can be viewed as the same thing.

 

The following menus are designed to assist in creating the survey (DTM) model.

 

1) Entering of data                        : ASCII Files, Fieldbook, Graphically, Digitizing.

2) Modeling the data                        : Search triangels, Adding triangles.

3) Saving the data                        : Files.

4) Producing a contour plan                : Output.

5) Examples                                : Example.
