MENU : Cad - Text - Move

 

Move the indicated text to a new position and/or rotate it around its origin. The position of the text will be referenced to by the text origin attribute. Use the Page up/Page down keys to rotate the text in one degree intervals. Use Ctrl Page up/Page down to rotate the text in 45 degree intervals.  

 

Multiple elements may be moved in one step. To select a group of text elements, keep the SHIFT key down while selecting the text. Press the Escape after the last element is selected to activate the Move process.
