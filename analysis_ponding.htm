MENU: Ana - Ponding                                

 

 

In the tab, select Ponding, circled below.

 

 

 

Ponding Menu

 

 

Output has 2 options.

 a) 'Change the color of ponding triangles to blue' will set the color of the triangles (of the active project) in the ponding area to blue. This will not change the dtm elevations.

 b) The Ponding points and triangles can also be copied to a new (empty) project so it can overlay on the original dtm. The elevations of        the points in the new project are set to the ponding water surface elevation.

 

 

 

 

Typical result :

 

a) The red dots are the ponding source points. This is the position where all the elevations around the point is at a higher Z value.

b) The yellow crosses are all the points that will fall within the full water surface.
