MENU : Irr - Shifts

 

ACT        

The colour identifying the blocks active in the selected shift. Click on the panel to change the colour.

 

OTH                

The colour identifying the blocks active in other shift. Click on the panel to change the colour.

 

DEAD                

The colour identifying the blocks not active in any shift. Click on the panel to change the colour.

 

Active number                

The number of blocks active in this shift is displayed.

 

Inc/Exc                

When clicking inside any block, the block will either be included or excluded from the active shift

 

Colour - Active                

Blocks active in this shift identified with the active colour.

 

Colour - Other                

Blocks active in other shifts identified with the active colour.

 

Colour - Dead                

Blocks not active in any block.
