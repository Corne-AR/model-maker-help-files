MENU : toE - All or Idside

 

Model Maker will automatically detect the open sides of triangles in the design project and create toepoints at regular intervals on the ground project. Add the toepoints to the data, or just view the toe-lines.

 

Toepoints on the identified sides will generate points  along the triangles of the design project only where the side of the triangle connects to 2 identified points.

 

Show toeline

The position of the toeline will be shown and no new points will be added to the database.
