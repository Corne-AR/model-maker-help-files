MENU : Vol

 

Use the functions in this menu to:

 

               Calculate the volumes of stockpiles or quarries.

               Calculate the area of a single contour.

               Calculate the water capacity of a dam.

 

               Note that the calculations are only performed on the triangular surface of the active project.

         The result will only be true if the DTM model yields a fill volume for a dump or a cut volume for a quarry.

         The methods in this menu is not suited to calculate a cut & fill combination volume.
