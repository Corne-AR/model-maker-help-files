MENU : Cad - Line - inFo                                

 

Report the length, bearing and co-ordinates of a line. You may change any of the values. Model Maker will automatically calculate the new co-ordinates and update the line.  

 

Line numbers 0 - 7 is displayed at the top left hand side of the screen.
