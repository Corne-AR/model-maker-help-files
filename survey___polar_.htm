MENU : sUr - pOlar

 

Calculate a co-ordinate of a new point. First indicate a point on the screen from which to calculate the new point and then enter the distance and direction to the new point.

You will be prompted for an elevation of the new point. If you do not wish to add the calculated coordinates to the DTM data base as a new point, press CANCEL the elevation dialog box.

The relative polar information will be displayed. Use the Editor functions to copy the information to the editor for further manipulation.
