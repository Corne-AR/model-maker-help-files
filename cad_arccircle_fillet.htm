MENU : Cad - Circ - filleT / fillet_liN                                

 

This function will generate a new arc as a fillet between an existing circle and another circle or line.

 

Fillet

 

Fillet an arc between an original arc and arc.

 

In the example, the base circle (a) and circle (b) will be filleted.

 

To create arc (c), first point to arc (a) at position (1), then point to arc (b) at position (2).

To create arc (d), first point to arc (a) at position (3), then point to arc (b) at position (4).

 

 

Fillet_lin

 

Fillet an arc between an original arc and line.

 

In the example, the base circle (a) and line (b) will be filleted.

 

To create arc (c), first point to arc (b) at position (1), then point to line (b) at position (2).

To create arc (d), first point to arc (a) at position (3), then to line (b) at position (4).

 

After the fillet has been placed, use the Cut arc or Trim arc options to clear up the picture where necessary.
