To use the function, you can Either use all the points or just the Identified Points.

 

The function will give you a report according to the simulation of the selected points.

 

The report and text copied to cad can either be in Meters or Millimeters.

 

 

 

 

If you select to Write difference to CAD the Elevation difference Between Points will be added as CAD text.

Underlined Text - Indicates area is over the 95th Percentile Limit (6.5mm FM2)  

Normal Text – Indicates the elevation difference within the E specification (6.5mm FM2)

Two text colors are used. For normal text the pen is the default pen (eg. pen 5) color in the text dialog box.  For underlined text the pen number is one more than the normal text (eg 5+1= pen 6).

 

Notes:

The survey points must be on a regular north/south, east/west grid.   Points on a rotated grid cannot be analyzed.

Rotate your survey if the points are not on such a grid.

Each point will be inspected relative to two other points—one to its right and above it on the grid.

Please note that the inspection occurs based on the X and Y coordinates and not on the sequence of the points.

There will be a Tolerance check of 100mm on the YX Grid and if the points exceed that tolerance the result will give an error message.

You will need to get the points within that tolerance to continue.
