MENU : Cad - Line - Add - Parallel

 

Enter a parallel line next to an existing one. The (P)en functions can be changed while you are entering lines. Press the "+" key to delete the last entered line.  

 

 

 

Multiple copy of dist 1

Enter a distance for DIST 1 and the number of repetitions in DIST 2.

Parallel lines @ DIST 1 will be generated DIST 2 times
