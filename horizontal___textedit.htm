MENU : Hor - Txtedit

 

The horizontal alignment data of a road may be edited, printed and setting out data may be calculated. This option is only available in module 7 of Model Maker.

 

Enter the alignment name and starting chainage.

 

Use the Files Open & Save buttons to transfer the data to disk.

 

Create new horizontal alignment by typing in the alignment data. You may specify a name for the alignment, the starting peg distance and the name, Y-, X-co-ordinate, radius, transition length in and transition length out for each point of intersection on the alignment.  

 

Go to the text editor.

 

Transfer the program to the dialog box that calculates and display the curve information like deflection angles and curve lengths.

 

View the calculated co-ordinates at regular intervals.

 

Go to the section of the program where setout and other join calculations are performed.
