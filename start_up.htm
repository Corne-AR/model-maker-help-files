WELCOME TO MODEL MAKER

 

Model Maker is a powerful engineering software

package that covers many aspects of civil

engineering and irrigation design.

 

The software has been in operation for many years. Starting off as a basic contouring package in the eighties it has grown over the years and progressed through the DOS era into a market leader in its field today.
