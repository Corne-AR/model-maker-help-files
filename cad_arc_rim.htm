MENU : Cad - Circ - trim_L / trim_A                                                

 

This function will extend the endpoint of a circle/arc to the intersection of a CAD line and the circle.

The radius of the arc/circle will be unchanged but either the start or end angle will be recalculated to the new intersection position.

 

To trim an arc, indicate close to the endpoint of the arc. Then indicate on the CAD line at the approximate intersection position.

 

Hints:

 

In this example the first image shows an arc cut by a line.

               To trim the arc to the line as in the second image, indicate the arc approximately at position 2, then indicate the line at position 1.

               To trim the arc to the line as in the third image, indicate the arc approximately at position 3, then indicate the line at position 1.

 

Press the '+' key  to undo the trim if the result is not what you want.

 

Trim - Arc to arc

This function works on the same basis as the trim to line option except that both elements are arcs or circles.
