MENU : Settings - Line & Text info

 

 

 

 

A panel may be activated that will continuously display the attributes of the default CAD line & CAD textelements. This panel will be situated just  below the manipulate plot menu at the top of the screen. Use the Settings menu (ALT S) on the top row of options to click the panel on or off.

 

Note:

You may click on this panel to activate the pen and textattribute boxes.

 

The colour of the menu represents the CAD element colour.
