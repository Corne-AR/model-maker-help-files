MENU : gXt - Vert - >>3D

 

Put the whole graphics picture in 3D mode. No editing of the elements is allowed. Used mainly for visual purposes. The DTM points are also displayed in 3D and the triangles and contours may be drawn. The vertical view angle and distortion factor may be entered.  

 

These variables control the 3D picture. Vert + - will add and subtract 1 degree off the vertical view angle. An angle of 0 is viewing the model from the side while a 90 degree angle is viewing the model from above. Distort + - will increase and decrease the vertical distortion factor by one.
