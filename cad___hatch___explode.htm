MENU : Cad - Hatch - Explode                                

 

Convert a hatch to normal CAD entities and manipulate it with the normal CAD functions.

 

Single

 

Explode an indicated hatch to CAD lines.

All

 

Explode all hatch elements on the drawing to CAD lines.

Once a hatch is exploded, the process cannot be reversed.

Identified

 

Explode all hatch elements on the drawing to CAD lines.

Once a hatch is exploded, the process cannot be reversed.
