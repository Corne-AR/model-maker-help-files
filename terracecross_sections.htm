MENU : teR - Cut-fill - X sections

 

The start and end positions of the center line need to be indicated on the screen The distance between the indicated points will be displayed. This gives you the chance to specify the chainage interval that sections must be taken, or the number of sections must be taken. Taking the sections close to each other results in a more accurate answer, but takes longer to calculate.

 

The volume will be calculated using the following procedure.      

*    At each chainage a cross-section is extracted from the design & ground models

*    These sections will then be compared to find the common cut and fill area between them. The areas on the left and right sides of the sections will be closed by joining the sections vertically at the point of the shortest section.

 

The end area method will be used to add the areas between sections when calculating volumes. No topsoil stripping or layerworks are taken into account. The volume is  between the surfaces without adjustments.
