MENU : toE

 

This menu has the functions to calculate toepoints between two surfaces.

 

         

 

Model Maker will produce new points at specific places where the batter slopes intersect with the ground.

The triangles defining the design platform must be loaded in a project, while those of the ground model must be in a different project.

The toe-lines will be calculated along the open sides of triangles in the design project.
