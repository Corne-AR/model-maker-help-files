MENU : Cad - Line

 

 

Lines

Lines are normally the element used most commonly in a CAD drawing. Many different details like fences, roads, property borders, rivers, power lines, telephone lines and houses are usually represented by CAD lines. Model Maker allows you to draw lines with different colours and line thickness.

Lines types can vary from solid lines to various types of dotted lines.

The user may also define line types
