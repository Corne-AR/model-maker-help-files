MENU : Tri - sortY

 

Sort triangles - triangles will be sorted from left to right. Sorted triangles increase the speed of calculations under certain circumstances. Sorting triangles is optional and not needed under normal situations.

 

To sort the triangles.

1.                Select Tri in the left hand side menu.

2.                Select sortY in the bottom menu.

3.                On the dialog, click Yes to start the sort process.
