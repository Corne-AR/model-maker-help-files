DEFINITION : Triangles

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

A triangle represents the surface between 3 DTM points. Model Maker uses this surface to extract information like contours, elevations, areas, volumes, etc.

A triangle cannot exist on its own. The DTM points from which the triangles were created  must be present in memory before the triangle can produce meaningful information.
