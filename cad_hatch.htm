Hatch

The hatch option allows you to define any area and to fill it with either a grid of lines or with a solid colour. Use this to easily identify and draw attention to certain areas.

 

Model Maker CAD supports an intelligent hatching system. Use up to 100 nodes to indicate an area for hatching.

Define one island per hatch area.

Each island can have up to 100 nodes.

Edit any of the hatch attributes after a hatch has been defined.

The system includes moving, adding and deleting nodes; adding and deleting an island; moving the entire hatch; and changing the hatch pattern and line type.  

 

Hint:

If you need to add more islands to a hatch and you are using an ink jet plotter do the following:

·Hatch the area in question.
·Add a hatches with a white fill color where all the islands must be.
·When the drawing is produced on a plotter the white hatches will define white areas in the original hatch thereby simulating the islands.
