DEFINITION : KOORD

 Top  Previous  Next

 

A KOORD station identifies a station in the dtm database that is opened/created to accept finally reduced coordinates. When ASCII yxz data is loaded, Model Maker automatically opens a KOORD station in the database and allocates the points to it.

 

A KOORD station behaves like a dummy station and does not occupy a position in the database. It is also not plotted on the drawing.
