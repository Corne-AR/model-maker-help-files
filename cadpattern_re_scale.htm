MENU : Cad - Patt - Rescale

 

Place patterns at a defined co-ordinate position and relative scale size. A scale size of 1 displays a pattern at the same size as it was developed. A scale of 2 will double its size and a 0.5 scale will half the original size.

 

Re-scale

 

Change the relative scale size after the patterns have been placed in position.

 

You may rescale selected patterns, rescale all the patterns with a defined name, or rescale all the patterns globally.
