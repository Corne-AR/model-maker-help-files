MENU : Cad - Patt - Info

 

When a pattern is placed on a drawing its position is defined by: the Y&X co-ordinates of the origin; the relative scale size and the rotation of the pattern.

 

Info

 

The values of these attributes are displayed. You may also change any of the parameters.

Editing the co-ordinates will display the pattern at the new position. The scale value will change the size of the pattern. The rotation angle will change the amount that the pattern will be rotated around the base co-ordinate.
