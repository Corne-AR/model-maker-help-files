MENU : Cad - Line - Special                                

 

Move the cursor over the graphic and when the appears press the enter button on the mouse and a more detailed explanation of the function in question will be given.

 

 

 

 

Div2+

 

Divides a line into multiple sections. Indicate close to the point on the line that must be divided.

 

Filllet

 

Indicate the first line to fillet 2 lines.

 

Chamfer

 

Indicate the first line to chamfer 2 lines.

 

Embank

 

Draw embankment lines to identified lines.

 

Blockmove

 

Move and/or rotate lines in a polygon area. To indicate a point use the trackline mode to follow a CAD line.  Keep the Shift key down to follow single points.

 

sQuare

 

Add the 2 opposing sides of a square.

 

2Circle-line

 

Add a line between 2 circles. Indicate on the circle boundary.

 

Bend_arc

 

Turn a line into an arc.

 

Spline

 

Indicate line No. 1 to spline a series of lines.  Keep the Shift key down to follow a polyline.

 

Drape

 

Allows you to elevate CAD end points to the DTM surface.
