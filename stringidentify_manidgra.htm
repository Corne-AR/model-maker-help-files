MENU : gXt - Id - Man-id - Grade

 

 

  EXAMPLE        

 

All the identified strings will be inspected and any node with an elevation of zero will be re-elevated (interpolated) based on the elevations of the closest nodes of the same string that have elevations other than zero.

 

The example looks at a string in the vertical mode. In the before picture, the nodes between node 4 & node 8 are at zero elevation. In the after picture, Model Maker has regraded nodes 5,6 & 7 to a straight line between the elevations of nodes 4 & 8.
