MENU : Cad - Circ - cUt                                

 

This function allows you to remove a portion from an arc/circle.

 

First, indicate the arc or circle you want to cut and then indicate the 2 point on the circle where the gap will be created.

 

         

 

In this example, section A of the circle was removed by first pointing to that segment and then indicating positions B and C on the circle. The cursor snap mode may be utilized and any format may be used to indicate positions B and C.

 

Normally the arc segment that is cut is then deleted from the drawing.

By keeping the SHIFT key down when pointing the the segment, Model Maker will still cut the circle/arc but the deleted segment will be retained.
