MENU : Vol - aVerage                                

 

The average elevations of all the points in a defined area will be calculated. This method of calculating a volume is often used when you have a large dump with uniform type height. You can calculate the volume by multiplying the area times the average height.

 

To indicate the area, use the standard area definition box.  

 

This information box appears once you have defined the area.

 

This takes you to the Area Calculation box.  When you exit from here, your information will be placed in the standard Editor.
