MENU : Vol - aReacon                                

 

Calculate the area covered by a specific contour elevation. If the contour is not closed, the start and end points will be joined with a straight line to form a closed area.

 

 

 

Example of a model that is not suitable for this method.

 

Plan view

 

 

 

This model is like a horseshoe and closing the contours on the side will result in the area being calculated that is not part of the actual material.

 

3D View

 

Notice the red cross, this is the area that the contour will calculate and incorrectly add "space volume".

 

Typical models like these must be calculated by means of the enhanced cut and fill methods.
