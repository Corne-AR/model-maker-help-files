MENU : Tri - Csearch

 

This is the fastest method of searching triangles. To increase the speed, triangles are not drawn while the searching is done, also this method cannot be interrupted and is mainly used to search triangles on large databases.

 

The CAD ID line functions activated in the search parameters are not applied by this method. Use the wipeout legs option under WIPEOUT to delete thin triangles on the side of the model, if necessary, after the triangle are generated.

 

When doing the Quick search, Model Maker places a big triangle over all the points and then subdivides the triangle into smaller ones until all the points have been triangulated. In order to speed up the process, the actual triangles are not displayed while the searching is in progress. The points already passed over is just colored white in order to display the progress.

 

You are allowed to stop the quick search before it is finished although the intention is that the QUICK SEARCH should be allowed to finish the complete model.

 

You will see that the NORMAL SEARCH  can be regulated via the  SEARCH RADIUS and ANGLE  as specified under  SEARCH PARAMETERS. This does not apply to the  QUICK SEARCH  which would cover the whole model irrespective of the distance between the points.
