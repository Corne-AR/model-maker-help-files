MENU : Cad - Line - Cut                                

 

Divide a single line into 2 separate elements.

 

First indicate the line to be cut. Then indicate the 2 new terminal points.

 

EXAMPLE:

 

 

In the picture, line 'a' will cut at the intersections of a & b  and  a & c.

Step 1 :                Select line 'a' by pointing to position 1.

Step 2 :                For the first cut point, set the cursor mode to Endpoint - Any and point to position 2.

Step 3 :                For the second cut point, set the cursor mode to IntersectionB (line & line) and indicate positions 3a and 3b.

 

The intersections with line 'a' will be calculated and look like this:

Line 'a' is cut and two new lines 'a1' and 'a2' are generated.
