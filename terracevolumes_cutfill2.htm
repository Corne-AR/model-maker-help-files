TERRACE : Volumes - Cut & Fill - Compactions

Enter up to 5 different compaction or bulking factors .

Model Maker will update the volumes and add the additional information to the volume printout.

The factor must be entered as percentages (%).

 

These factors are treated as follows:

100% means that 1 cube cut volume is 1 cube fill volume. A value less than 100% means that the fill is compacted by the difference between 100% and the value. For example, to specify a compaction factor of 20%, enter the value as 80.

Bulking is entered as a value greater than 100. A 30% bulking factor will be entered as 130.

The factors are applied to the volume in the following manner.

 

EXAMPLE:

Cut = 200m3 and fill=100m3 with a 20% compaction

Result : Cut=200*(100-20%)=160m3 and fill=100m3

 

Cut = 200m3 and fill=100m3 with a 20% bulking

Result : Cut=200m3 and the fill=100*(120%)=120m3
