Menu : Cad - Circ - Hemis                                

 

This option flips all the arcs in the drawing from the survey format (south and clock-wise) to the Cartesian format (east and anti clock-wise).

Hints:

This is often necessary after a dxf file is read from programs operating in a different co-ordinate system.
