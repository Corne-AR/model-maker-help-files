Model Maker can add 7 different types of dimensions.

The SURVEY and ANGULAR dimensions are controlled by the parameters in this dialog box.

 

Number of decimals

A survey dimension consists of the length and the angle between the 2 indicated terminal points. You may specify the number of decimals for display purposes.

 

Decimal Notation

It is often necessary to change the decimal notation of the dimension displayed. Specify the character to be used when indicating the decimal place in the length and angle text.

 

Angle Format

360 Format                : The angle will be given as an absolute value from the zero direction.                

In NE,NW etc.                : The absolute angle is calculated, but displayed in a format where the deviation from the 4 main quadrants are                                  determined. The following example uses the southern hemisphere orientation as base.

 

         Direction                        NE,NW

         45                                S 45 W                        (Meaning 45 degrees west of south)

         190                                N 10 E                        (Meaning 10 degrees east of north)

         260                                N 80 E                        (Meaning 80 degrees east of north)

 

Dimension Info

Only length                : Only the distance between the 2 terminals is displayed.

Only angle                : Only the direction between the 2 terminals is displayed.

Both                                : Both the items are displayed.
