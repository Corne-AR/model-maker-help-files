MENU : Vol - Contours                                

 

Contours are drawn at contour intervals specified in the plot parameters. The area of each contour polygon will be calculated and the volume of the dump determined by the end-area method.

 

 

 

The area of open contours will be calculated by joining the start and end points of the contour with a straight line.

The 2 red open contours are closed by the blue lines in (3) to calculate the area. The green contour yields a correct enclosed area.

The brown contour in (1) and (2) is unpredictable because the blue closing line cuts through the contour resulting in the incorrect calculation of areas 1&2.

This method of calculation may start to become unreliable if the base of the model is very irregular or if many of the contours are open sided.
