MENU : Cad - Patt - Rename

 

Use this option to rename patterns that are part of your cad data to other patterns what are available on disk.

This is normally necessary when you receive a KCD file, referencing certain patterns from somebody else but you do not have the same patterns with the same names on your computer.

 

In the example above the KCD file has 2 patterns defined. The pattern called AIRVALVE is available on disk and will plot on screen. The pattern called CATCHPIT is defined in the KCD file but cannot be found at the indicated folder position.

 

 1 

Pattern is available and will plot.

 2 

Pattern must be renamed. To rename, click on the text.

 3 

a. The name of the pattern to be renamed will be shown here.

b. Click this button to allow you to select another pattern from disk.

c. Click this button to delete this pattern.

 4 

This is the folder where MM will be forced to look for patterns. Usually in a scenario where MM is used on a network between a few users.

 5 

This is the folder where MM can alternatively look for patterns. Usually in a scenario where your patterns are in different locations.

Press the button and choose and alternative folder.

 

If you do not want to rename patterns or the patterns are not important, either switch the display of the patterns off or delete them.
