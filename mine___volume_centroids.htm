MINE : Volume Centroids

MENU : Min - vol_Move - vol_Centroids

 

Determine the weighted average position of the bulk cut or fill points. Generate the file used for this exercise with the terrace cut & fill option. This file consists of points labeled CUT or FILL and Model Maker will accumulate the elevations (the depth between the design and original surface) of the points within a defined area and draw a circle onto CAD at the centroid with the total amount of volume as text in the centre.

Use this function to determine mass haul distances between the cut & fill earthworks areas.
