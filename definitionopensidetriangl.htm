DEFINITION : Open side triangle

Top  Previous  Next

 

A single triangle always consists of three corner points and three sides connecting at the corners. If two triangles lay next to each other, they would have one leg in common. A whole network of triangles would have many shared triangle sides. However, the triangles on the perimeter of the model would have at least one side that is not shared by another triangle. These are open sides triangles.

 

Use the side function to display the perimeter of the model.
