MENU : Tri - eRrors

 

Model Maker will search for possible overlapping triangles. See in the example below.

 

 

Triangles could overlap as a result of a point being moved on a model where triangles already exists. It is important to correct this, as this will affect all the general data such as contours, cross sections, volume slope analysis etc.

 

To check for errors.

1. Select Tri in the left hand side menu.

2. Select erRors on the bottom menu.

3. A dialogue will appear stating whether the triangles are correct or incorrect.
