MENU : Irr - Shifts

 

This function will automatically assign the irrigation blocks into a pre defined number of shifts.

 

 

To use the function do the following:

       Determine the number of shifts you are allowed to use and enter it into the

               appropriate edit box.

       Click the update button.

 

Irri Maker will allocate the blocks to a shift number based on the total volume of water that

each shift will carry.

This is done by taking the following into consideration:

 

       All the blocks should have approximately the same flow.

       The distribution should be in such a way that the major pipes are not really overloaded during the operation of individual shifts.

 

When the update button is pressed, Irri Maker goes ahead and assign the shifts based on the above parameters.

 

Note:

The existing shifts numbers are re-arranged after the update button has been pressed and cannot easily be reinstated. If you

have gone to great lengths to do the block shift

allocation yourself and only want to try the automatic function out as a test, first save your irrigation design file to disk. If you then do

not want to make use of the automatic defined shift numbers, recall the saved design from disk again.

 

If you press the close button without having pressed the update button, your original shift numbers will not be altered.

 

       This automatic function does not really do a hydraulic analysis of the design, nor are any pipe diameters considered. The result is achieved by purely looking at the layout of the design and the total flow volume in the different pipes. There is always a good chance that you may manually improve on the allocation of blocks after the hydraulics have been calculated and the actual flows and pressures in the system are available.
