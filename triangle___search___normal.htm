MENU : Tri - Csearch

 

Search for triangles - MM starts the automatically searching based on search parameters. Searching takes place on the screen and you will be able to follow the progress. You can interrupt the process in order to change the radius, or search angle, or to abort the search. An average radius is displayed giving an indication of the distance at which new triangles have been found.

 

It is a good practice to have the radius set at about 1,5 times that of the average. A too large radius will unnecessary slow down the searching progress, whilst a too small radius will leave gaps in the model or even stop the searching prematurely. New triangles may be automatically added to the existing model. MM scans the whole model trying to find where new triangles may be added to existing open sides according to the set search parameters.
