DEFINITION : Tachy

 Top  Previous  Next

 

 

Tachy data refers to the data format of a complete survey field book. Points surveyed in tachy format will always belong to a tachy station. A tachy station is the position where the surveyor has placed his survey instrument at the time when all the spot shots were recorded. The station would hold the co-ordinates, elevation, instrument height and horizontal correction angle
