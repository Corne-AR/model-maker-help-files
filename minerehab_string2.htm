MENU : Min - Rehabilitation - rehab-String

 

The DTM points of the active model will be remodeled based on the following:

 

1) A string must be indicated that will define the boundary within which the remodeling will take place.

2) All the DTM points of the active project will be inspected. Points falling within the area defined by the boundary string will be re-elevated based on a formula where the elevation is calculated from 2 identified strings based on opposite sides from the DTM point.
