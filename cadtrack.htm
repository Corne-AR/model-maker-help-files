MENU : Cad - Track

 

Automatically generate the positions of selected distances on an oval track.

 

 

The track will always consist of

A                The home straight

B                The last curve

C                The back straight

D                The first curve

and must be indicated in this order when defining the elements.

 

The set of 4 elements must meet each other at the end points for Model Maker to continue with the calculations.

The red bar indicates the finish line (0 distance) and will automatically be taken at the common point between line A and curve D.

Up to 4 requested distances may be entered at a time. Enter a distance of 0 if not all 4 inputs are to be used.

 

Indicators

The meter positions may be indicated by a tick line (normal CAD line) and /or the label (normal CAD text) of the distance value.
