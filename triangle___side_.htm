MENU : Tri - Side

 

Function

 

The open sides of the triangles will be drawn, indicating the limits of the model. Press any key on the keyboard or a mouse button to continue.  

 

Purpose

 

To inspect the model for open gaps (missing triangles) left within the model and to see that the area is covered by the triangles as intended.

        Defining the color of the side.

 

Note:

Make it a habit of viewing the open sides after the model is finalized. Remembered that if a toeline is to be calculated, it will probably be done on the outside of your model. If there are gaps, then MM also calculates a toeline around these gaps.

 

Depending on the search method used for defining the triangle, network gaps may or may not be created.

 

To use the side option.

1. Select Tri in the left hand side of the menu.

2. Select Side in the bottom menu.

3. The sides of the Triangles will flash, press enter on the mouse, the sides of the triangles will be displayed in a different colour.
