MENU : Cad - Line - Special - Div2+                                

 

Divide any line into 2 or more parts.

Indicate the line that must be divided. The menu lets you divide the line by 3 different methods.

 

 

Information

 

Line Length / Direction 1>2

The information box indicates the existing length of the line and the direction from the start to end points.

 

Segment #

 

Enter the number of segments. Model Maker calculates the length of each segment. In the example, 2 segments are requested. The original line of length (509.616) is being divided in 2 (254.808) segments.

 

Segment Length

 

Enter the segment length. Model Maker calculates the number of segments. The line will be divided and the last segment have the length of the calculated remainder, if any. In the example, the (50.962) shows 50 full segments (10 in length) with a remainder of 9.62.

 

User lengths

 

Enter variable segment lengths. If the total length of the entered segments is shorter than the total line length, a remainder will be generated.  The old line will be replaced by the new segments even if the total length of the entered segments is longer than the original line.

 

DO

 

Model Maker CAD will perform function 1 or 2 or 3 based on the DO button.

Remember that, when identifying a line, the endpoint closest to the cursor position will always be PT1. The division of the line will always start from PT1 as the fixed point.
