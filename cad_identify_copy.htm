MENU : Cad - Id - Man-id - Cop

 

Copy all the identified CAD entities to a new position.

 

 

1.                You will be prompted to indicate a reference point. Then, copy the entities in a straight line, in rotation, or to all identified survey                points.

 

2.                To copy on a line, will you be prompted for a point to copy to and for the number of times to copy.

 

3.                To copy with rotation, you will be prompted for an angle. The entities will be copied around the reference point.

 

4.                Select all identified tachy points to copy all the identified CAD elements, for example a tree drawn in the CAD, to all the identified                survey points.
