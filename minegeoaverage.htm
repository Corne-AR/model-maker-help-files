MENU : Mine - Volume - Elevations

 

Use this function only if Geo areas have been defined. Every geo area is inspected and every mining layer is activated in turn. The elevations on a regular grid within the geo area boundary is added up and the average elevation recorded to a spread sheet file.

Remember that the elevations of the dtm points can be substituted with any other index, eg., grades, and this average would then denote the average grade within the geo area.
