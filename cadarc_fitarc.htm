Menu : Cad - Circ - Fit arc

 

This function will reposition the arc based on the layout of the identified DTM points.

 

       

 

In  the above example the arc that has to be fitted between the survey points was first placed, then the controlling survey points were identified. The Fit Arc option was then selected and the specific arc was indicated. Based on the weighted averages of the distances between the points and their intersections on the arc, the arc's new balanced position was determined and then placed at those coordinates.
