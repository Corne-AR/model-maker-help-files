MENU : Cad - Hatch - Island                                

 

Add Island

Place an island in a hatch.

 

A single island, defined by up to 100 nodes, may be placed within a previously defined hatch. To Add island, first identify the hatch in which the island must be placed. Then, identify the nodes of the island in the same way as the hatch was defined.  

 

Delete Island

Point to the inside of the island to be deleted.
