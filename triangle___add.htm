MENU : Tri -Add

 

A new triangle may only be added to an existing triangle with an open side.

However, if no triangles exist, then indicate the first three points for a triangle.

 

An open leg is the side of a triangle where there is no neighboring triangle, or where it is not common to any other triangle.

The side option identifies an open leg.

First point to the inside of an existing triangle with the cursor closest to the leg common to the new triangle.

Then point to the third point to which the new triangle must fit.

 

You cannot create a new triangle that crosses the lines of other triangles.
