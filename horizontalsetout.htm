MENU : Hor - Txtedit - Setout

 

Calculate the distance and bearing from a setup position to each peg distance, other PI points and the BC and EC of each curve on the alignment.

Specify the co-ordinates of your setup station and some orientation points.

 

Double click on either the setup at, or orientate to, inputs. Model Maker will then allow you to pick a co-ordinate from the points on the graphics screen.

 

The distance and bearing from the setup station to the specified PIs, including  BC and EC and all the peg distances within a specified radius, will be calculated and printed to the editor.

 

You may also display the lines from the setup point to all the calculated points. These lines can also be copied to CAD.
