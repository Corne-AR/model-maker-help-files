MENU : Vol - X_sections                                

 

Extract cross-sections either vertically (south to north) or horizontally (east to west) through the model. The starting and ending points of each section will be joined to form a closed area. The areas of the X-sections will be added by end-area -method, to calculate the dump or quarry volume.
