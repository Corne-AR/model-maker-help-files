MENU : Cad - Dim - eXplode                                

 

Dimensions exist as separate elements in the CAD environment. Although they are made up of text and lines, these elements are part of the dimension and cannot be edited by the normal text and line functions.

 

By exploding the dimension, the elements within the dimension are released to CAD and added to the text and line database where they can be manipulated by the general CAD functions.

 

 

Single

Explode individual dimensions indicated by the user.

 

All

Explode all the dimensions on the CAD drawing.

This function cannot easily be reversed once the dimension is exploded.
