MENU : Cad - Line - Special - Fillet                                

 

 

Filllet two lines with an arc, unless they are parallel lines.

 

 

 

Indicate both lines :  Point to the endpoint of each line where the arc is to be placed. Enter the arc radius.  The arc will change the endpoints of the two lines and trim them to the tangent points on the arc. The arc will be drawn in the current pen attributes set by the Pen button. Press the '+' key to undo the last fillet. You will then be asked if you want to repeat the exercise with different arc radius.

 

Place cursor in the Enter fillet arc radius box and press the short cut key F9.  This takes you to the Model Maker calculator:
