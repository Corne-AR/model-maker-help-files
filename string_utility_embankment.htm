MENU : gXt - Utility - Embank

 

Embankment hatch lines can automatically be drawn between 2 strings.

The distance between the hatch lines & the length of the lines are specified in the parameter section of the embankment.

These new embankment lines are generated as normal CAD lines and manipulated with the CAD features.
