MENU : Cad - Line - Special - sQuare                                

 

Form a square based on two existing lines by copying these 2 lines to the other sides of the square.

Use this function when any three points of a square are available.

 

 

Draw the 2 lines between the three points. The square function automatically copies these 2 lines to the opposing sides to form the square.

 

EXAMPLE:

Points 1,2 & 3 are available. Draw lines A and B. Use the square function - indicate lines A and B (See the cursor crosses). Lines B2 & A2 will be automatically generated from points 2 & 3 to meet at 4.
