MENU : sUr - Fitline

 

Take any CAD line and place it in a new position based on a best fit between all the identified DTM points of the active project.

 

Identify the correct DTM points before the function is used. After the function is selected, point to the CAD line that must be placed in a best fit position.

 

Best fit.

Model Maker will use all the identified DTM points and go through many iterations where the weighted averages of the points to the left and to the right of the CAD line is calculated. The position of the CAD line will each time be adjusted until there is less than 1% difference between the 2 sides.
