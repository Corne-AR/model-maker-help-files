This function will generate an ellipse between 4 points.

 

 

In this example the 4 indicated points are shown at the endpoints of the orange lines. A hypothetical rectangle (green dotted) is then determined from the 4 points and the ellipse is generated inside the rectangle.

 

NOTE:

 

The green rectangle in this example is shown only for illustration. In practice the square will not be drawn and is not necessary.

 

The 4 points must be shown in order, left - right, bottom - top.

 

The ellipse will always be developed north to south, not at an angle.

 

The resulting ellipse is a polyline.
